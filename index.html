Denys & Misha — Brothers in Design

From Kharkiv, Ukraine to Toronto and Cologne — two brothers, one creative force.

Ukrainian Roots

Born and raised in Kharkiv, Denys and Misha were always full of energy and curiosity. Denys played competitive football (soccer), dreaming of joining Dynamo Kyiv, while Misha trained in karate, earning regional titles before the age of 13. These early passions shaped their discipline, drive, and teamwork.

A War That Changed Everything

With the outbreak of war, Misha relocated to Cologne, Germany, while Denys was already establishing a life in Toronto. Despite the distance and loss, their connection remained unshaken.

Rebuilding Through Design

Reunited online, the brothers focused their talents into 3D modeling, CAD drafting, and digital design. They launched 21 Fusion Talent — a design studio working with architects, manufacturers, and custom interior builders across Europe and North America.

What They Do

Denys leads the Toronto division, specializing in architectural drafting, custom furniture renderings, and 3D visualization. Misha handles European clients, focusing on industrial design, animation, and prototyping.

Together, they provide services to:

  • — Interior Designers & Decorators
  • — Manufacturing Firms
  • — E-commerce Brands & Visual Agencies
  • — Furniture Startups

Future Goals & Partnerships

The brothers plan to expand into digital assets and online design education. They are currently developing a YouTube channel, monetizing tutorials, and creating a digital product library for designers worldwide.

Partnerships underway:

  • — Autodesk affiliate program
  • — RenderHub collaboration
  • — Ukrainian Tech Diaspora Network

Brothers, Bound by Vision

Denys & Misha continue to grow, not only in skill and business, but in legacy — a story shaped by resilience, rooted in Ukraine, and shining far beyond.
